FAQ

Questions, answered

Quick info on estimates, inspections, scheduling, and commercial service.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes. We can often estimate by phone for standard cleaning, and we’ll schedule a walkthrough for larger jobs or regulated sites.

Do fumigation and pest services require inspection?

Most of the time, yes—pricing depends on access points, severity, square footage, and any site-specific requirements.

What should I do before the crew arrives?

We’ll send a simple prep list. In general: secure pets, clear countertops (if requested), and provide access to the areas being serviced.

Do you service commercial properties and property managers?

Yes. We can provide documentation, checklists, and recurring schedules for offices, warehouses, and multi-unit properties.

How do I book?

Call us or use the contact form. We’ll confirm scope, scheduling, and any walkthrough requirements before service.
